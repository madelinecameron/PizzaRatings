Qualifications

I eat a lot of pizza.

Digiorno Rising Crust - Cheese08/31/2019
400°F for 21 minutes Paired with: Diet Cherry Coke

Easy to cut, very stringy cheese, dough is soft and chewy. Crust is sufficiently crusty with a nice crunch without being teeth-shatteringly hard. Sauce is understated without a strong note of tomato. Cheese is present, but not overbearingly so. Cheese has a tendency to come off in chunks.

Overall, it is a satisifying pizza to eat.

Sam's Club Thin Crust - Cheese09/03/2019
425°F for 13 minutes Paired with: Water

Easy to cut, crust underneath is slightly browned. Sauce has a distinctive taste, almost like school lunch pizza. The cheese is somewhat stringy, but not thick, satisfying strands. The cheese strands are fine and short. The cheese is not bad, it has a slight taste but it isn't off-putting.

The cheese stays on throughout consuming. The outside crust is mildly crunchy, but not cracker-like you expect from a thin crust pizza.

The pizza also includes what I assume are mozzerella slices on top. The slices are alright but their inclusion doesn't improve or take away from the pizza.

Overall, this pizza is a pizza. It's not great, but not the worst pizza.
